Our Story

Furniture Born from
a Love of Craft

Furniro was founded in 2008 by two furniture restorers who believed the world deserved pieces designed to last — not just for a season, but for a lifetime. What began as a small workshop in Porto has grown into a globally recognised brand, yet our soul remains rooted in that same workshop philosophy.

Every piece is the result of close collaboration between our in-house designers and the skilled artisans who bring them to life. We source only sustainably certified wood, ethically produced fabrics, and responsibly mined metals.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by a commitment to craft, people, and the planet.

Sustainability

FSC-certified wood, water-based finishes, and fully recyclable packaging. Our factories run on 100% renewable energy by 2026.

Master Craftsmanship

Each piece is shaped and finished by hand. Our artisans average over 12 years of experience and sign every item they complete.

Built to Last

We offer a 10-year structural guarantee. If it breaks under normal use, we repair or replace it — no questions asked.

Customer First

Our dedicated team is available 7 days a week with free in-home design consultations in all major cities we deliver to.

Fair Trade

We partner only with suppliers who pay fair wages and maintain safe working conditions, verified by independent annual audits.

Timeless Design

We reject fast-furniture trends. Every collection is designed to age gracefully and fit naturally into any interior for decades.

Frequently Asked

Quick Answers

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5–10 business days within Europe. Large items are delivered by our white-glove team who will also assemble the piece.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free returns window. Items must be in original condition. We arrange free collection at a time that suits you.

Do you offer custom sizing?

Yes! Most pieces can be made to measure. Visit our showroom or contact us. Lead time for custom orders is 4–6 weeks.

Are your products sustainably sourced?

All wood is FSC-certified, fabrics are OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 certified, and our packaging is 100% recyclable and plastic-free.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times are calculated at checkout based on your location.

How do I track my order?

Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link by email. You can also log into your account for real-time delivery updates.
